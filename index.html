Biography

Vijay Kumar is a Ph.D. Candidate at the Department of Mathematics, Clarkson University in Potsdam, NY-USA. His research interest lies in Applied Mathematics, Statistics, Air-Quality modeling, Data Science & Machine Learning. He is currently working on Air-Quality modeling, predicting, and analyzing public health factors including Air Pollution.
